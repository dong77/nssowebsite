Who we are

Our culture is built on our core values

Northern Star & Southern Ocean is a private markets investment firm. By applying the skills of our specialised investment teams to a variety of investment opportunities, including ones that are harder to find and harder to manage, we aim to capture a complexity premium for our investors, provide them with additional diversification benefits and help them to achieve sustainability and/or impact related objectives. We invest in a range of private market asset classes, through coming-led funds and customised private asset mandates.
